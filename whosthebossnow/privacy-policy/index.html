Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

This Privacy Policy explains how the game “Who’s The Boss Now” (developed by KeTongSeng) collects, uses, and protects your information. Your privacy is important, and we are committed to ensuring that your data is handled safely and transparently.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Data

The game itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). However, certain third-party services used within the game may automatically collect non-personal device data, such as:

  • Device model & operating system
  • Country/region (for ad localization)
  • In-app activity related to advertisements
  • Crash reports or performance data

1.2 Third-Party Services

This game uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob (for displaying advertisements)

Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads.

You can read Google’s privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. How We Use Information

The game uses information solely to:

  • Display ads and reward players after watching ads
  • Improve user experience
  • Maintain app stability and performance

No personal data is sold, shared, or used for tracking outside of ad delivery functions.

3. Children's Privacy

This game is intended for general audiences but does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

4. Data Security

We do not store personal data on our servers. All data processed by third-party services (such as Google AdMob) is protected according to their security practices.

5. Your Choices

Users can disable personalized ads at any time by modifying ad preferences in their Google settings. You may also limit data collection by restricting device permissions or enabling “Opt out of Ads Personalization”.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
